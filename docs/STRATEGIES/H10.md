## Strategy Logic

Harvest volume from stop-loss clusters after an impulse move.

**Conditions (all required — TZ-056 formalization):**
- **C1 Impulse:** max sweep across any window in `{2,3,4,6,8,12}` consecutive hours ≥ **1.5%**  
  (window immediately precedes the consolidation, non-overlapping)
- **C2 Consolidation:** price held corridor ≤ **2.5%** range for **6–48** consecutive hours ending now;  
  last close not at boundary (within 0.2% of edge)
- **C3 Bilateral zones:** liquidity zones weight ≥ 0.5 both above *and* below current price,  
  at **0.7–4.5%** from current price (±5% map coverage, top_n=60)

**Action:**
- Identify heavier bilateral zone (target_zone)
- Place limit grid: `grid_steps=6` orders at `0.25%` spacing from zone center
- Max position: 0.15 BTC total
- TP: 0.5% above avg_entry (long) or below (short)
- Time stop: 2h if no TP
- Optional protective stop at -0.5% to -1.0% from avg_entry

## Known Limitations

1. Liquidation component = 0% in backtest (collectors < 3 days old at time of run). In live, liquidation data improves zone quality from day 3+.
2. TZ-053a ran with relaxed C1=1.2%/C2=1.0% to get 150 setups; TZ-056 restores strict 1.5%/2.5% and rebuilds the 6-48h scan — full 2-year rerun to be done locally by operator.
3. Volume harvested from grid is 0.15 BTC × 2 (entry + exit) per cycle. At BTC ~95k → $14.25k/cycle turnover.
4. No slippage model. Limit orders assume perfect fills at touch.
