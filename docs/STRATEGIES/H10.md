# H10 — Liquidity Grid Probe

**Status:** MVP backtest complete (TZ-053a, 2026-04-29)  
**Mode:** backtest only; live deployment pending TZ-053b

---

## Strategy Logic

Harvest volume from stop-loss clusters after an impulse move.

**Conditions (all required):**
- **C1 Impulse:** max sweep in last 2-4 bars >= 1.2% (relaxed from original 1.5%)
- **C2 Consolidation:** last 3 candles all mutually overlapping, total range < 1.0% (relaxed from 0.8%)
- **C3 Bilateral zones:** liquidity zones >= 0.5 weight both above (0.7-2.0% from current price) AND below

**Action:**
- Identify heavier bilateral zone (target_zone)
- Place limit grid: `grid_steps=6` orders at `0.25%` spacing from zone center
- Max position: 0.15 BTC total
- TP: 0.5% above avg_entry (long) or below (short)
- Time stop: 2h if no TP
- Optional protective stop at -0.5% to -1.0% from avg_entry

## Known Limitations

1. Liquidation component = 0% in backtest (collectors < 3 days old at time of run). In live, liquidation data improves zone quality from day 3+.
2. C1/C2 conditions relaxed vs original spec to get 150 setups (was 12 with strict params).
3. Volume harvested from grid is 0.15 BTC × 2 (entry + exit) per cycle. At BTC ~95k → $14.25k/cycle turnover.
4. No slippage model. Limit orders assume perfect fills at touch.
